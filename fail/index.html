ARTificial INTELLIGENCE FAIL

Failed AI generated art.
Sister site to the Art Intelligence gallery.

Images are generated in the style of real-life artists using a large database of artists with details about the methods, genres, styles, subjects, locations, and time period of each artist.

Unfortunately, it sometimes goes horribly wrong.

? failed images in the collection.

View recent failed images - https://artintelligence.fail/recent.html

A viewer to display the most recent failed images. Clicking the right side of the screen displays the next oldest image. Clicking the left side displays a newer image until the newest image is reached.

Shuffle failed images - https://artintelligence.fail/shuffle.html

A viewer that shuffles failed images from the entire collection at random. A new shuffled image is loaded every five minutes. Clicking the screen will pull up another shuffled image.
